Who We Are

The Sweetwater Film Club members are made up of anyone interested in the creation of videos and cinema. This includes anything to do with sound, lights, camera, and action.

Sound

Great audio is a vital part of storytelling, and our club welcomes anyone excited about exploring, experimenting with, and collaborating on all aspects of film sound to enhance our films together.

Lights

At the Sweetwater Film Club, we are a passionate community dedicated to exploring and mastering the art of cinematic lighting, discovering how light, color, and technique can transform storytelling and create captivating visuals.

Camera

We are a community dedicated to mastering the art and science of cameras and cinematography, exploring every aspect of image capture and visual storytelling to create stunning, collaborative films.

Action

At the Sweetwater Film Club, we are a supportive community of aspiring directors and passionate actors dedicated to collaborating, learning, and honing our crafts to create compelling and unforgettable cinematic experiences.
